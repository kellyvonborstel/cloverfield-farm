About Cloverfield Farm

Our farm is located in Aumsville, Oregon and has been family owned and operated since 1988. At Cloverfield Farm, we respect the land we care for and the people who raise your food by following the highest national organic standards. Our produce is always free of pesticides and other chemicals, so it not only tastes great, but is always safe for you and your family.

We’re committed to selling high-quality organic fruits and vegetables at reasonable prices. Growing a variety of nutrient-dense foods and selling only what’s in season, we can keep costs low and quality high, and make fresh, healthy food affordable throughout the year.
