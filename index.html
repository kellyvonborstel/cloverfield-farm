Fresh Produce Delivery

Cloverfield Farm grows a variety of organic produce and delivers it right to your door. We’re a family-owned business located in Aumsville, Oregon, delivering daily to Salem and the surrounding areas. Because we only offer what’s currently in season, you can count on it being freshly picked, nutritious, and delicious!
