Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you deliver?
We deliver to Salem, Turner, Aumsville, Stayton, Sublimity, Mill City, and the surrounding areas.
What if I’m not home to receive my delivery?
Most of our customers are not home to receive delivery. It’s up to you where you would like us to leave your produce box. If you prefer we not leave it at your front door, we are bonded and will accept keys for gates, garages, apartments, and homes.
On hot days, how will my produce stay fresh?
On hot days, we keep delicate, heat-sensitive items refrigerated and only place them in your box when we get to your door. If your delivery cannot be placed inside or in the shade, we recommend that you leave a cooler with ice packs to keep delicate items fresh.
Is there any minimum number of deliveries I must commit to?
Of course not! You’re welcome to try a single delivery. If you have a subscription, you may cancel service at any time. We request give us at least 48 hours notice before your next scheduled delivery.
May I temporarily pause delivery without canceling my service?
You may opt out of any number of deliveries and recieve credit for future deliveries. Simply contact us at least 48 hours before your next scheduled delivery and tell us how long to hold your deliveries.
How fresh is your produce?
The produce you receive will be picked no more than 48 hours before it arrives at your door.
Do you guarantee your service?
Yes. If you’re dissatisfied with any produce you receive, please contact us immediately, and we will replace the item or offer you a refund. We stand behind our produce and delivery service one hundred percent.
